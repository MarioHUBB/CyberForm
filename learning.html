Password Strength Checker

The password strength checker helps you understand the security of your passwords. It analyzes:

  • Length and complexity of the password
  • Use of lowercase, uppercase, digits, and symbols
  • Detection of common patterns and dictionary words

Passwords are evaluated using entropy, measured in bits. Higher entropy indicates stronger, more secure passwords that are harder for attackers to guess.

The page generates strong passwords by randomly combining lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols according to your selected criteria, ensuring high entropy and resistance to guessing or brute-force attacks.

Encryption / Decryption Tool

The encryption tool allows you to securely encrypt and decrypt text. Encryption converts your data into a secure format that cannot be read without the proper key, while decryption restores it to its original form.

This demonstrates important cybersecurity concepts:

  • Symmetric key encryption
  • Safe handling of sensitive information
  • Client-side vs server-side encryption considerations

Using this tool helps understand how data protection works in real-world applications, like securing passwords, messages, and personal data.
